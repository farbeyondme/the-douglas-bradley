About Doug

Doug Bradley's journey from accounting, finance, and software to the heart of Dallas's bar scene is anything but typical.

After earning a BA in Accounting from the University of Northern Iowa, Doug built a successful career in finance before founding Dynatron Software, Inc., where he developed innovative web-based solutions for the automotive service industry.

After selling his software company, Doug and his wife Rachel took on a new challenge: building the End Zone brand from the ground up. Through strategic planning, operational discipline, and relentless learning, they proved that success can be built and repeated in virtually any industry.
