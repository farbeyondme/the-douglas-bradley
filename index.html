About Douglas Bradley

Douglas Bradley is a Repeatable Entrepreneur whose career spans software, hospitality, consulting, and real estate — four industries, four different business models, and one unifying operating system.

He began his entrepreneurial journey in 1997 when he founded Dynatron Software, a business-intelligence company that grew from a two-person startup into a nationwide SaaS provider serving thousands of automotive dealerships. After selling the business in 2016, he shifted into hospitality, acquiring and transforming four underperforming bar and grill locations into the End Zone Bar & Grill brand.

Operating the restaurants remotely from Florida, Bradley built centralized systems for leadership, financial oversight, and operations. He later applied the same principles to real estate through DBRB Homes, proving that clarity, disciplined execution, and repeatable systems can work across industries.
